A shark is a powerful, streamlined predator found in oceans around the world. Known for their sharp teeth and keen senses, they are expert hunters, feeding on fish, marine mammals, and sometimes invertebrates. Sharks are essential to maintaining balance in marine ecosystems.

Sharks are carnivores that feed on fish, marine mammals, and other sea creatures like squid and crabs. Larger species may hunt seals and dolphins, while some, like whale sharks, filter-feed on plankton. Their diverse diet helps maintain balance in marine ecosystems.
