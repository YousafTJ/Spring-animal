A lion is a powerful, majestic big cat known for its muscular build, golden coat, and iconic mane. As apex predators, they live in social groups called prides and are native to the African savannah.

  • Weight:
    • Male lions: 150–250 kg (330–550 lbs)
    • Female lions: 120–180 kg (265–400 lbs)
  • Age:
    • In the wild: 10–14 years
    • In captivity: Up to 20–25 years
  • Height:
    • At the shoulder: 1.2 meters (4 feet) for males, slightly less for females
  • Bite Force:
    • Around 650 psi (pounds per square inch)
