The ice bear, or polar bear, is a large predator native to the Arctic, with thick white fur and strong swimming abilities. It primarily hunts seals and is highly adapted to life on the sea ice.

  • Weight:
    • Adult males: 350 to 700 kg (770 to 1,540 lbs)
    • Adult females: 150 to 250 kg (330 to 550 lbs)
  • Age:
    • In the wild: 15 to 18 years
    • In captivity: Up to 30 years
  • Height:
    • Standing on all fours: 1 to 1.6 meters (3.3 to 5.
